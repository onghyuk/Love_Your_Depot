Love Your Depot LocusX

Love Your Depot is a new institution that supports the life of artwork based on its physical reality.

see more

We reserve judgement on works of art until our artists have enough time to understand their works and decide the destiny of each work.

We provide quality facilities and resources to support the special process of art production and the respectful disposal procedure.

We study and create prominent strategies to keep and sustain the body of art withTeamDepot.

TeamDepot members propose creative archives and develop interesting contents about the works and artists in our depot.

TeamDepot continuously posts online contents and collaborates with our artists for offline contents to interpret and promote each work.

We run membership equally to all the artists regardless of qualifications.
